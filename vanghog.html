Striva

A haunting yet hopeful journey through art and the mind, where creativity battles inner demons.

Gogh, a young artist struggling with MDD, retreats to his dusty attic, where his forgotten artwork lies. Overwhelmed by depression, he breaks down and falls into a deep sleep, falling into a vivid daydream. There, he faces three manifestations of his inner demons: the Whisperer, representing self-doubt; the Grey Fog, representing isolation and loneliness; and Hollow, symbolizing society’s criticism. With the help of Lumen, a spirit who represents his lost passion, Gogh fights back using his art (his paintbrush, color palette, and creativity) to defeat each adversary. When he awakens, he feels a renewed sense of purpose and picks up his brush once more, ready to reclaim his passion again.

Reclaim your passion. Paint your story.
